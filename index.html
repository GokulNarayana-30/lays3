Lay's is a brand of potato chips with different flavors,
         as well as the name of the company that founded the chip brand in the United States. 
    The brand is also referred to as Frito-Lay because both Lay's and Fritos are brands sold by
     the Frito-Lay company, which has been a wholly owned subsidiary of PepsiCo since 1965.
Frito-Lay primarily uses the brand name "Lay's" in the United States, 
        and uses other brand names in certain other countries:Walkers in the UK
